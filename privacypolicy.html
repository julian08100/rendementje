Last updated: April 21, 2026 Rendementje stores your portfolio data locally on your device using Apple SwiftData. What we collect: - Transactions and asset data you add or import. What we do not do: - We do not execute broker or exchange orders. - We do not place trades. Third-party services: - Market prices and metadata may be fetched from third-party finance APIs. Analytics: - Rendementje uses Hotjar (via Contentsquare) for anonymous session analytics, heatmaps, and usage insights to improve the app experience. - All monetary amounts, balances, and financial data are automatically masked and are never visible to Hotjar/Contentsquare. - No personally identifiable financial information is shared with analytics providers. Your control: - You can delete all transactions from the Transactions screen. - You can remove the app to delete local storage. This app provides educational and informational tooling, not tax, legal, or investment advice.
